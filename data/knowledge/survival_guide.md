# Bro Eden Survival Guide

## Community Purpose

Bro Eden is an adult community for gay, bi, and queer men. It is community
focused rather than a hookup server. Members are encouraged to socialize, get
to know people, read channel context, and respect the rules and quirks of each
space.

## The Gateway and Verification

The Gateway is the limited section available to Unverified members. To access
the rest of Bro Eden, a member must complete the server's ID verification
process confirming that he is at least 18 years old. Until verification is
approved, the member remains limited to the Gateway and its verification
resources.

## Staff and Support Tickets

Members can learn about the staff team in the staff information area. If
someone causes discomfort in DMs or public spaces, or if a member witnesses
rules being broken, they should create a support ticket for staff review.
Reports are handled confidentially and respectfully. Support tickets may also
be used for server questions.

## Levels and NSFW Access

Members gain XP by participating naturally in chat. Spamming or abusing the
leveling system can result in levels being rolled back. Level 5 unlocks the
server's access-controlled NSFW areas. The level requirement exists to help
members socialize before receiving those privileges and to protect community
privacy.

## Useful Starting Areas

Members can customize profile roles, post an introduction, share an
appropriate selfie, join the main SFW lounge, and use the designated NSFW chat
for adult conversation. Channel names and organization may change, so members
should follow current channel descriptions and staff direction.

## Random Accounts, Verification, and Scams

Before responding to an unexpected account, check whether the person is a
verified member of Bro Eden. Use caution with unverified or unfamiliar
accounts.

If a known member suddenly claims that an account was reported or asks someone
to sign in through a link, do not follow the link. The account may be
compromised. Report suspicious messages through a support ticket.

## Eden Credits

Members can earn server currency through daily rewards, work commands, games,
and events. Credits may be used for server shop items and perks. This system
does not override conduct, spam, or participation rules.

## Hiatus and Leaving

Members who need a break can ask staff through a support ticket for the
Hiatus role, which limits server access without requiring them to leave. Server
levels may reset when a member leaves, including as a privacy and NSFW-access
safeguard.

## Birthdays and Community Perks

Members can register birthdays through the designated bot command and may
receive a birthday role, community shoutout, XP boost, and server credits.

## Moderation Context

Mature language, adult jokes, and NSFW discussion are not automatically rule
violations in this 18+ community. Staff should consider the channel, consent,
roles, boundaries, targeting, persistence, and impact on other members.
Sexual content in SFW areas, unsolicited sexual contact, harassment, privacy
violations, illegal content, or disregard for a member's stated boundaries may
require intervention.
