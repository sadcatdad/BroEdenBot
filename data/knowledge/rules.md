# Bro Eden Server Rules

## 18+ Verification

Bro Eden is an 18+ server. Unverified members can access only the limited
Gateway section. Members must complete ID verification confirming that they
are at least 18 years old before receiving access to the rest of the server.
Underage users are removed.

## Respect and Community Conduct

Members are expected to use adult common sense, courtesy, and respect. Do not
harass or bully others, incite drama, bait conflict, target members with
disrespect, or create a persistently hostile or toxic atmosphere.

## Illegal and Prohibited Content

Do not post illegal content. Prohibited material includes gore, bestiality,
scat, necrophilia, loli or shota pornography, cub pornography, child sexual
abuse material, and similar illegal or exploitative content.

## Unsolicited Links and Spam

Do not send members unsolicited links or spam, including through direct
messages.

## Direct Messages and Boundaries

- Check members' profile roles and respect whether their DMs are open or
  closed, and whether they accept nude content.
- Get to know members in server chats before sending friend requests or DMs.
- Do not fish for DMs with hookup-style posts or use the server as a hookup
  dump.
- A friendly response or compliment is not permission to send nudes or
  unsolicited DMs.
- Do not become angry when someone does not reply or call them out publicly.

## Community Etiquette and Sensitive Topics

Bro Eden is intended to be an inclusive safe space. Topics involving rape,
suicide, extreme self-deprecation, pedophilia, racism, homophobia,
transphobia, fat or age shaming, or slurs require serious care and may be
inappropriate or harmful depending on context.

Religion should be discussed only in designated channels. Politics and world
events should stay in designated areas and remain civil. Do not attack people
for their beliefs or preferences.

The community is not a substitute for professional therapy. Members may seek
support in designated venting spaces, but persistent conduct that negatively
affects others may require staff intervention. Read the room and avoid intense
public behavior that appears toxic or drives others away.

## NSFW Access and Media

- Adult discussion and mature language are allowed in appropriate spaces.
- Pornographic media belongs only in designated NSFW channels.
- Non-pornographic NSFW media may be allowed in designated adult general
  spaces, with spoiler tags when appropriate.
- Self nudes and private nudes of other people, including private dating-app
  photos, do not belong in general chats.
- Self-nude content belongs only in the designated, access-controlled NSFW
  spaces.
- NSFW art may be allowed in the designated art area when spoilered.
- Never save, repost, or distribute members' nudes inside or outside the
  server.

## SFW Channels

SFW channels should remain non-sexual. Selfies and self-care photos should not
include intentional bulges, erections through clothing, pubic exposure, or
other overtly sexual presentation. Casual shirtlessness may be acceptable when
it is not sexual. Blatantly horny or sexual conversation should move to the
appropriate NSFW channel.

## Self-Promotion

- Money-making sexual platforms such as OnlyFans, Fansly, or related
  promotional accounts may not be advertised except in specifically allowed
  spaces.
- Such links may remain in a member's profile, but should not be posted as
  server spam.
- SFW projects may be shared where appropriate; ask staff when uncertain.
- Do not promote other Discord servers.

## Enforcement and Reporting

Staff may issue proportionate warnings and remove members when problems
continue. If a member is uncomfortable or witnesses rule-breaking, the proper
reporting path is a confidential support ticket.
